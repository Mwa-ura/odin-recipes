Chapati Madodo recipe ingredients

Ingredients

This meal need the least item to prepare.

  1. Wheat flour
  2. Beans
  3. Cooking oil
  4. Onions
  5. Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Sort your beans and soak them overnight in lukewarm water and boil them until they are tender and put aside.
  2. Fry the onions till they are golden brown, then add the ginger paste and tomatoes.
  3. Once the tomatoes are dissolved add garam masala, salt, carrots, stir until everything is thoroughly combined.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes after adding water.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with coriander.
  6. Serve with chapati

Thats it!
