Mayai boilo

Ingredients

This is the most simplest and my favorite meal. You only need two item and two steps to get started and to have your meal ready tp eat.

  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

I was kidding.

  1. Take your eggs and water put them in the pot
  2. Heat the pot until the eggs harden
  3. Remove the eggs from the pot cool them. And they are ready!
